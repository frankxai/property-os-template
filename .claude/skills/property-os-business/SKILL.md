# Property OS Business Skill

Use this when creating an offer, proposal, partner kit, sales page, implementation package, pricing ladder, or operating retainer for Property Intelligence OS.

## Offer Ladder

1. Template license: public-safe starter, docs, validation, sample portal.
2. Guided install: owner intake, property page, renter portal, listing drafts, release gate.
3. Premium install: custom brand, real media pass, database, email, weekly review.
4. Agency license: multi-owner operating model, white-label docs, team workflows.
5. Managed operations: monthly review, FAQ updates, listing refresh, support triage, integration monitoring.

## Rules

- Sell reduced admin, premium presentation, safer AI drafting, and faster setup.
- Do not sell legal, tenant-screening, accounting, tax, or auto-publishing outcomes in v1.
- Require owner approval for pricing, availability, lease, urgent repair, applicant, and publication decisions.
- Show implementers how they earn money through setup fees, retainers, customization, training, and white-label maintenance.

## Proposal Output

Include buyer, property count, current pain, package, deliverables, timeline, owner responsibilities, excluded scope, risk gates, and next action.
