# Listing Channel Checklist

## Own Website

- approved headline
- approved description
- photos approved
- availability note approved
- inquiry CTA tested

## Kleinanzeigen

- German headline
- German body
- rent and costs confirmed
- contact policy confirmed
- publication is manual

## ImmoScout24

- required property fields reviewed
- energy information handled by owner
- rent, deposit, and fees confirmed
- photos approved
- publication is manual or approved API route

## Immowelt

- required fields reviewed
- exact address policy confirmed
- owner checklist complete
- publication is manual or approved API route

## Universal Blockers

- missing rent
- missing availability
- unclear lease terms
- unapproved photos
- private address policy unresolved
- legal wording unresolved
