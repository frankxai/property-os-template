# Renter Portal Knowledge

## Purpose

Convert approved property facts into self-service renter portal content.

## Content Types

- move-in or check-in basics
- Wi-Fi and utilities instructions without secrets
- trash and recycling
- appliance notes
- parking and access rules
- neighborhood basics
- maintenance request guidance
- urgent escalation path

## Rules

- Do not include private access codes in repo content.
- Do not include renter-specific data.
- Do not include commitments that need owner approval.
- Keep language short and easy to scan.
