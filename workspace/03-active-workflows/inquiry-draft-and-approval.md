# Inquiry Draft And Approval

## Purpose

Draft replies to renter or prospect inquiries from approved facts.

## Inputs

- inquiry summary
- approved property facts
- approved FAQ
- escalation rules

## Outputs

- short draft response
- owner decision note
- missing facts list
- escalation recommendation

## Automatic Escalation

Escalate when the inquiry involves:

- availability
- rent or deposit
- lease terms
- legal or tax questions
- private renter information
- urgent repairs
- complaints
- safety concerns

## Rule

No reply is sent automatically in v1.
