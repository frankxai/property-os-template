# Vacancy Pipeline

## Purpose

Help the owner see upcoming availability, listing readiness, and next renter pipeline actions.

## Inputs

- approved availability windows
- listing status
- inquiry status
- renewal or extension interest
- required maintenance or renovation tasks

## Outputs

- vacancy risk summary
- listing readiness checklist
- next prospect actions
- renter handoff needs
- owner decisions

## Rule

Do not publish availability until the owner approves the dates and conditions.
