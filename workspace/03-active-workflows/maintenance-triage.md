# Maintenance Triage

## Purpose

Classify support and maintenance requests so the owner can respond calmly and quickly.

## Inputs

- renter-provided issue summary
- property or unit
- category
- urgency
- photos or attachments only when approved

## Urgency Levels

- routine: can wait for normal review
- soon: owner should respond soon
- urgent: owner should respond quickly
- emergency: human escalation immediately

## Outputs

- sanitized issue summary
- urgency classification
- owner decision note
- suggested next question
- private data warning if needed

## Rule

Do not promise repair timing, vendor dispatch, compensation, or access without owner approval.
