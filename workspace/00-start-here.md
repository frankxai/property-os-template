# Start Here

## Purpose

This workspace helps a property owner manage approved facts, listing drafts, renter information, support questions, and weekly decisions.

## What AI May Support

- summarize property facts
- draft listing copy
- draft renter FAQ answers
- triage maintenance requests
- prepare owner review notes
- identify missing information

## What AI May Not Do

- publish listings
- promise availability
- set rent or fees
- make lease, refund, legal, or tax decisions
- send renter-facing messages without approval
- handle emergency routing without human oversight

## Current Active Workflow

Start with `workspace/03-active-workflows/listing-draft-studio.md` unless the owner chooses another workflow.
