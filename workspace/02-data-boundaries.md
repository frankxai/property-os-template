# Data Boundaries

## Red Data

Never use in public/template repos or general AI tools:

- renter names tied to addresses, payment, complaints, or lease terms
- identity documents
- bank details, IBANs, deposits, or payment records
- access codes, lockbox codes, alarm codes, Wi-Fi passwords, or utility account numbers
- private owner financials
- private maintenance disputes tied to identity

## Yellow Data

Use only in approved private contexts:

- renter preferences
- stay or move-in details
- maintenance history
- availability windows before publication
- internal pricing notes
- vendor notes

## Green Data

Public-safe when accurate and approved:

- public property descriptions
- approved amenities
- general house rules
- neighborhood notes
- generic FAQ answers
- workflow templates

## Approval Required

Owner approval is required for:

- renter-facing messages
- listing publication
- pricing or availability
- lease terms
- maintenance commitments
- urgent escalation routing
- public screenshots or proof
