# Renter Support Playbook

## Normal Questions

Use approved knowledge articles first. If the answer is missing, draft a response and ask the owner to approve it.

## Sensitive Questions

Escalate to the owner for:

- pricing
- lease terms
- refunds
- legal wording
- complaints tied to identity
- access information
- private address details

## Urgent Questions

Escalate immediately through the owner-approved urgent channel. The system can collect context, but should not promise repair timing or safety resolution.

## Improve The System

Every repeated question becomes a candidate FAQ.
